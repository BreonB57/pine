FAQ

Will these tags work on all smartphones and tablets?

NFC tags will only work on NFC equipped devices. Most Android smartphones are equipped with it, however currently Apple's devices do not support NFC on services apart from Apple pay.

How secure are the tags? Could someone hack into them?


Our tags are encrypted meaning nobody can edit the contents of the tag and the data stored on it. If you would like yours reprogrammed, you can send it back to us.

What can I use it for?

Some possible uses for the tag are:
A sticker on your wall which lets guests connect to your wifi by tapping their phone on it.
A virtual business card, where when tapped on a client's phone, can add a contact with your details.
NFC can also be a good way to easily show people a YouTube channel or website extremely quickly.
